Goals, Future Plans, Directions:

Website goals

  • Provide a place where anyone can set up a business and trade with anyone in the world at no cost
  • Combine aspects of ebay, amazon, facebook, twitter, google and wikipedia.

Volunteer to help us make these sections:

  • HELPS Blog, comment, communication section where people can post information about how to start a business, do-it-yourself training, start manufacturing, start exporting, start selling
  • SALES A selling section where you can search for a product and buy it
  • AUCTIONS
  • TRADES
  • MANUFACTURERS A production section, where one can list manufacturing, production, or quantity goods for sale
  • LOCATION A geography section, where one can specify their locale
  • FREE A free section where people can list things that are free
  • SERVICE / SKILL A service section where people can list their skills or work for hire (make a fireplace, photograph your baby, paint your house, create a work of art.)
  • Create an open contribution format so that people can add function as they wish and then list those functions and links on the general site – patterned after gnu and other open systems.

Dedicated to facilitating trade around the world with a free exchange of ideas, respect for all peoples, and sustainable life.
